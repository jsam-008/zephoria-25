About the Institution

The event, Zephoria'25, is proudly organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) of Vel Tech High Tech Dr. Rangarajan Dr. Sakunthala Engineering College. The department is dedicated to nurturing innovation, technical excellence, and creative thinking, providing students with the skills and platform to excel in both academic and practical pursuits. With state-of-the-art facilities, expert faculty, and a focus on cutting-edge technologies, the department ensures that every event, project, and workshop reflects a perfect blend of learning and innovation. By hosting Zephoria, the department continues its tradition of encouraging students to explore, innovate, and showcase their talents on a grand stage, making learning both engaging and transformative.

About us

Zapped with Ideas, Zinged with Joy!

Step into Zephoria, a 2-day celebration of talent, creativity, and innovation! With a dynamic blend of technical and non-technical events, participants compete, collaborate, and showcase their skills in an atmosphere buzzing with energy, inspiration, and friendly rivalry. From coding battles, project expos, and quizzes to dance, music, and creative performances, every corner is alive with ideas, excitement, and a touch of magic. ✨ Here, every idea is celebrated, every skill recognized, and every moment is filled with learning, joy, and discovery. Participants not only showcase their talent but also connect, network, and grow alongside some of the brightest minds. At Zephoria, every participant leaves with the pride of having been part of something extraordinary — where brilliance meets fun, ideas spark into reality, and memories are made to last a lifetime! 🪄💫

Join us at Zephoria — where brilliance meets fun, ideas come alive, and unforgettable memories are made! ✨💫
